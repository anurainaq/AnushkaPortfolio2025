Wine
Label

Inspired by the opulence and drama of the Baroque art era, this project reimagines classical grandeur for a modern wine brand. The aim was to explore how historical art movements can shape contemporary design identities.

The label design captures Baroque’s signature ornamentation, contrast, and sense of movement, while translating it into a refined, luxurious aesthetic suited for today's market.

This project was an exercise in learning through style translation understanding how the visual languages of different eras can be revived and reinterpreted to speak to modern audiences.

SUTRA

Sutra is a mock brand identity celebrating upcycling, circular fashion, and mindful design. Rooted in sustainability, it transforms discarded fabrics into timeless, handcrafted pieces—from crochet accessories made of T-shirt yarn to custom upcycled garments. Blending Indian cultural aesthetics with modern minimalism, Sutra's identity uses Devanagari-inspired typography and symbolic motifs like hangers and leaves to express its values of renewal and connection. Through story-driven branding and community engagement, Sutra makes eco-conscious fashion both personal and accessible.

Billboard
Design

This project reimagines the Romanticism era's ideals of emotion, individuality, and nature through a modern lens by creating a billboard for a fictional dating app. Rather than drawing on the word "romance," the concept delves into the era's cultural essence—its celebration of authentic emotion, self-expression, and human connection. The design translates Romanticism's painterly textures, dramatic contrasts, and organic forms into a bold, contemporary visual narrative, emphasizing genuine emotion over superficial connection. The project explores how historical movements can inform modern communication design, using art history to deepen the storytelling of everyday brands.
